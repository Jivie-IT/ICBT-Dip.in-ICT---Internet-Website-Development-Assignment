Our Story

Established in 2020, Subara Bitesu is Colombo's premier destination for authentic Japanese cuisine with a Sri Lankan touch. Founded by Chef Hiroshi Yamamoto, our restaurant blends traditional Japanese culinary artistry with the vibrant flavors of Sri Lanka, creating a unique dining experience that honors both cultures.

Our Vision

To be the bridge between Japanese culinary excellence and Sri Lankan hospitality, where every meal tells a story of cultural harmony and gastronomic innovation.

Our Mission

We commit to:
- Serving authentic Japanese dishes using locally-sourced ingredients
- Preserving traditional cooking techniques while embracing innovation
- Providing warm, attentive service that reflects Japanese omotenashi
- Creating a welcoming space that celebrates both cultures
