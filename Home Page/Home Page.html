Welcome to Subara Bitesu

A Journey of Flavor, Fusion, and Culture At Subara Bitesu, we don't just serve food - we craft experiences. Born from a deep love for two rich culinary traditions, our restaurant is a cultural crossroads where the fiery soul of Sri Lanka meets the refined artistry of Japan. This is fusion cuisine reimagined - bold, exciting, and uniquely ours.

The Subaru Bitesu Experience

From the moment you step inside, you're immersed in an atmosphere that blends modern Asian design with warm, island energy. Bamboo textures meet tropical greens. Japanese minimalism meets vibrant Lankan rhythm. Our chefs, trained in both culinary traditions, bring passion and precision to every plate. Using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and house-made sauces, we create dishes that feel familiar yet excitingly different. Our menu ranges from signature fusion rolls and fiery ramen with Ceylon curry infusions, to sushi platters with tropical twists, spicy rice bowls, umami-packed street bites, and sweet treats that blend mochi with Lankan jaggery.

Our Philosophy

We honor the Japanese principle of "shun" - using ingredients at their seasonal peak. Our sushi rice is prepared with Sri Lankan red rice for a unique local twist, while our sashimi features the freshest daily catch from Sri Lankan waters.
